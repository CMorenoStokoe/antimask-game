COVID-19 Anti-Vaxx: The Game

This game demonstrates how Anti-Vaccination movements could cost lives during the COVID-19 pandemic.

The COVID-19 vaccination will help achieve herd immunity to keep everyone safe, especially those most at risk.

It is important to vaccinate as many people as possible because you can carry COVID-19 to other people.

This game is designed to show how you can infect others around you, even people you've never directly met.

How do I win?

Your goal is to spread an infection from the start in one town to another town at the end.

How to play

Press an area to infect it.

You can only infect areas next to areas you've already infected.

You won

Well done you infected the next town!...

However, the government has noticed your infection and will start vaccinating people.

Hint: It will be too difficult for you to infect vaccinated areas!

Infection contained!

Good job but you were only able to infect a few areas because everyone around you was vaccinated! .

Vaccinations combined with mask wearing stops diseases from spreading droplet and aerosol particles

You might need a little help to keep your infection going...

Meet Joe.

Joe will refuse his vaccination because he doesn't think vaccines work.

Find and infect Joe!
Thank you for playing!

If Joe had accepted his vaccination, the infection would have stayed in just a few areas and not spread further. Hopefully this game has helped demonstrate how vaccines protect everyone, including people we've never met.
