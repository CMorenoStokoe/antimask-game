Anti-Mask: The Game

This game demonstrates how if you don't wear a mask you can infect people, even if you've never met them.

Your goal is to spread an infection from the start at one town to another town at the end.

Anti-Mask: The Game

How to play:
Press an area to infect it.

You can only infect areas next to areas you've already infected.

You won

Well done you reached the next town but the government has noticed your infection.

The government will now start making people wear masks to prevent you from infecting them.

However, Joe is on your side. Joe is not exempt from wearing a mask, he just doesn't want to wear one. You can always infect him.

Trapped!

Everyone around you are wearing masks so you can't infect anyone else. Try again!

Masks are not a replacement for social distancing but they do make it much harder for you to be infected.

Anti-Masker

When you wear a mask you limit droplet and aerosol particle spread when you breath, talk, cough and sneeze:

This is especially important indoors and when you are close to people. You can infect people even if you have no symptoms.

Joe isn't wearing his mask so you can infect his area through droplet and aerosol particles.

Thank you for playing!

If Joe had worn his mask, the infection would not have been able to spread. Hopefully this game has helped demonstrate how masks protect everyone, including people we've never met.
